Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Avanzo Cyber Institute's policy on course refunds?

We offer a refund policy under specific terms and conditions. If you're not satisfied with your course, you may request a refund within a designated period after enrollment. Please contact our support team for detailed guidelines on eligibility and the refund process.

2. Does Avanzo Cyber Institute provide assistance with job placement after course completion?

Yes, we are committed to supporting your career goals. While job placement is not guaranteed, we offer career assistance that may include resume building, interview preparation, and guidance on job search strategies to help you enter the cybersecurity workforce.

3. Are the courses at Avanzo Cyber Institute self-paced, instructor-led, or both?

Our programs are designed to accommodate different learning preferences. We offer both self-paced courses for flexible learning and instructor-led sessions for a more guided, interactive experience.

4. How long can I access the course materials after enrolling at Avanzo Cyber Institute?

Once enrolled, you will typically have access to the course materials for an extended period. The exact duration depends on the course type and format. Full details are provided upon registration.

5. Is it possible to switch from a self-paced course to an instructor-led course after enrolling at Avanzo Cyber Institute?

Yes, switching from a self-paced course to an instructor-led format may be possible, depending on course availability and your progress. Please reach out to our support team to explore your options.

6. What certifications will I receive upon completing the course?

Upon successful completion, you will receive a certificate that validates your skills and knowledge in the subject area. Some programs may also prepare you for industry-recognized certifications.
